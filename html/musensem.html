MUSEN 210 Campus Philharmonia Orchestra (1, max. 15) VLPA
Orchestral opportunity for non-majors. Meets once weekly, rehearses under the direction of the Masters and Doctoral orchestral conducting students. Serves as a laboratory for the conducting students. Performances are community outreach events which occur once per quarter. Major string, chamber orchestra, and symphonic repertoire rehearsed and performed.
View course details in MyPlan: MUSEN 210
